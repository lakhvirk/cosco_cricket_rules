Winnipeg Cricket Tournament Rules

```

Player Registration & Eligibility

Team Composition

  • Players per team: Maximum 16 players (must register one day before tournament)
  • Minimum players to start: 7 players required at match time
  • Player exclusivity: Players cannot be registered with multiple teams (both teams disqualified if violated)
  • No additions: New players cannot be added once tournament begins

Fielding Assistance

  • Teams with 7-10 players may use fielding help from other team players
  • Fielding help allowed must be inside 30-yard circle

Match Schedule & Timing

Arrival & Punctuality

  • Arrival time: Teams must arrive 30 minutes before scheduled match
  • Grace period: 5 minutes after previous match ends
  • Penalty: Late teams forfeit match to opponents

Match Duration

  • Regular matches: 5-8 overs per side
  • Semifinals & Finals: Minimum 8 overs per side
  • Weather adjustments: Overs may be reduced due to weather conditions

Bowling Rules

Bowler Restrictions

  • Minimum bowlers: 5 different bowlers must be used
  • Maximum overs per bowler: Based on over
  • Ball bouncing: One bounce allowed, second bounce = no ball

Field Restrictions

  • Powerplay: First 2 overs only
    • Maximum 2 fielders outside inner circle during powerplay
    • Maximum 5 fielders outside inner circle after powerplay
  • Leg-side restriction: Maximum 5 fielders allowed on leg side (bowlers side does not matter)
    • 6 fielders on leg side = no ball

Batting & Dismissal Rules

LBW & Runs

  • LBW: Not applicable in cosco tournaments
  • Byes & Leg-byes: Both allowed
  • Run out crease change: Allowed if batsmen crossed halfway before dismissal
  • Catch out crease change: Not allowed, even if halfway crossed

Dead Ball Conditions

  • Ball bounces twice before popping crease
  • Batsman not ready and doesn't offer shot due to distraction
  • Ball bounces after popping crease = live ball

Equipment Standards

Bat Specifications

  • Standard bats only: No modified or "Fatta" bats
  • Maximum length: 38 inches (excluding toe stone)
  • Toe stone: Maximum 0.5 inches for bat protection only
  • Hitting area: No stones or modifications allowed
  • Inspection: Umpires must check bats before play

Match Balls

  • Each innings starts with 2 new balls (team's choice)
  • Only burnt balls to be used

Wide Ball & No Ball Rules

Wide Ball Standards

  • Called according to standard wide ball guidelines
  • On-the-line deliveries: Considered wide
  • Batsman movement: Shuffling doesn't affect wide call

Combined Infractions

  • Wide + high bounce = wide only (not both)
  • Wide + no ball = no ball (not both)

Game Management

Wicketkeeper Changes

  • Teams must notify umpire before changing wicketkeeper
  • Failure to notify = no ball penalty

Time Management

  • Between overs: Maximum 1 minute
  • New batsman: Must be on crease within 2 minutes of dismissal
  • Innings break: Maximum 5 minutes
  • Time wasting penalty: 5 runs awarded to opposition

Noise & Disturbance

  • First offense: Warning for audible disturbance during bowler's run-up
  • Second offense: No ball penalty

Tournament Administration

Match Fixtures

  • Fair draw system conducted publicly or via video call
  • No team receives preferential treatment

Weather Policy

  • Matches may be postponed or rescheduled based on conditions
  • Tournament cancellation only if:
    • Weather prevents full day of play
    • Rescheduling exceeds budget constraints
    • Insufficient teams available for makeup dates

Umpire Authority

  • Final decisions: Umpire's decision is binding in all cases
  • Player conduct: Physical violence results in tournament ban

Awards & Recognition

Individual Awards

  • Most Valuable Player (MVP)
  • Top Batsman
  • Top Bowler

Award Eligibility

  • Same player cannot win multiple awards
  • If MVP is also top performer in batting/bowling, second-place finisher receives that award

These rules ensure fair play, efficient tournament management, and an enjoyable experience for all participants.
```
